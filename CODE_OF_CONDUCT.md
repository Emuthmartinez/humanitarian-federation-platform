# Code Of Conduct

This project supports humanitarian response work. Contributors are expected to
act with care, patience, and respect for people under stress.

Do not use issues, pull requests, or discussions to publish private crisis data,
harass affected people, spread unverified rumors, or pressure volunteers into
unsafe disclosure.

Maintainers may remove content or block contributors who endanger people,
publish sensitive data, or repeatedly ignore the privacy and life-safety rules
in this repository.
